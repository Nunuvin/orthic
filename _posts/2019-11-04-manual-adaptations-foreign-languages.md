You can now [read about using Orthic to write languages other than English]({{ site.url | append: site.baseurl | append: "/manual#application-to-foreign-languages" }}). The languages covered are French, German, Italian, Latin, and Greek. It's remarkable how few changes are needed to adapt an orthographic system to another language, especially when compared to a phonetic system.
